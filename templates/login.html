Know Your Seller

Introducing a way to stop scammers...

This is a site designed for users to rate sellers of EDM tickets. Write reviews about your transactions and run background checks on ticket sellers & promoters!

  • To look up a seller, copy the url to their Facebook page and paste it in the search bar.
  • If you've ever had a great experience with someone, write a nice review. We want the good sellers to be rewarded for their honesty and professionalism.
  • If someone has ever stolen your money or given you fake tickets, let the world know!
  • To change your review about someone, simply review them again. Only your last review will count.
